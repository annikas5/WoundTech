WHAT ARE THE KEY STEPS IN WOUND CARE, START TO FINISH?

1
Introduction: Greeting and briefing patient/visitors
2
Assessment: Observing, manipulating, measuring, and documenting the wound
3
Setup: Preparing materials and patient
4
Removal: Taking off old dressings
5
Cleaning: Removing debris and contaminants
6
Treatment: Applying topical agents or equipment
7
Dressing: Packing/covering the wound and securing it
8
Conclusion: Debriefing with patient/visitors
